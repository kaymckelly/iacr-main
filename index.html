Versions of IACR website refresh

Round two demos (two of them)

After the first round of candidates, we narrowed it down to two choices (which we refer to as v1 and v2).

NOTE: There is also a search demo, but v2 has search in it. There is also a list of all available images here

Version v1

  • Wide layout.
  • Hover dropdown on menus on desktop
  • Several versions of footer
  • Search not completed yet

Version v2

  • Two different variations on the nav bar.
  • Both searches work with server-side ajax.
  • A few lingering bugs on less-used browsers.
